# Forge Limits and Non-Claims

## What Forge Does NOT Do

**Not AI-Generated:**
- Uses rule-based logic only, no LLM calls
- Cannot discover new physical mechanisms
- Cannot propose solutions outside adjacency knowledge base

**Not Exhaustive:**
- Adjacency list is hand-curated (~20 pairs)
- May miss relevant technique transfers
- Domain coverage optimized for surgical robotics

**Not Validated:**
- Hypotheses require expert validation
- Does not guarantee novelty
- May suggest known solutions

**Not a Replacement for Expert Judgment:**
- Outputs inform but do not replace expert analysis
- Requires domain expertise to interpret
- Does not make design decisions autonomously

## What Forge Does NOT Claim

- **Novelty:** Does not guarantee hypotheses are novel
- **Feasibility:** Does not verify physical feasibility
- **Completeness:** Does not find all possible solutions
- **Safety:** Quality gate catches obvious issues but may miss subtle ones

## What Forge Does Claim

- **Rule-Based:** Deterministic, auditable hypothesis generation
- **Testable:** Every hypothesis includes testable prediction and falsification test
- **Traceable:** Full provenance linking hypotheses to Spine signals
- **Safe:** Quality gate prevents unsafe suggestions (0% dangerous suggestion rate target)
