THE REASONING SPINE
This is the full decision trace for the last action. Five nodes, always in order.
OBSERVE — what the system saw (actuator state, safety mode, consent)
DERIVE — what it concluded from observations
ASSUME — what it took for granted (treaty version, policy binding)
DECIDE — the governance verdict: ALLOW or BLOCK, with reason
ACT — what actually happened (state change or no-op)

Every node is individually hashed. The chain is tamper-evident.
