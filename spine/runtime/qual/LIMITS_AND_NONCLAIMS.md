# SPINE Limits and Non-Claims

## What SPINE Does NOT Do

**Not a Simulator:**
- Does not perform FEA, CFD, or multi-body dynamics
- Does not solve differential equations or run time-domain simulations
- Does not model fluid flow, heat transfer, or structural deformation

**Not AI Prediction:**
- Uses deterministic constraint checking, not machine learning
- Does not learn from data or adapt to new failure modes
- Does not predict outcomes probabilistically

**Not a Replacement for Expert Judgment:**
- Outputs inform but do not replace expert analysis
- Requires domain expertise to interpret results
- Does not make design decisions autonomously

## What SPINE Does NOT Model

- Full finite element analysis (FEA)
- Computational fluid dynamics (CFD)
- Real-time control system dynamics
- Multi-body kinematics/dynamics
- Material microstructure evolution
- Complex failure propagation paths

## What SPINE Does NOT Claim

- **Clinical Validity:** Not validated for clinical use
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Does not ensure FDA/CE compliance
- **Exhaustive Coverage:** Does not catch all possible failures
- **Perfect Accuracy:** May miss subtle or novel failure modes

## What SPINE Does Claim

- **Structured Pre-decision Analysis:** Systematic constraint evaluation
- **Traceable Reasoning:** Full reasoning chains for every output
- **Machine-Checkable Constraints:** Deterministic, auditable checks
- **Honest Uncertainty Reporting:** Clear confidence and evidence types
- **Zero False Positives:** No false alarms in test suites (0% FP rate)
